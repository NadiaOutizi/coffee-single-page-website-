what makes our coffee special?

There’s more to speciality coffee than the looks of it. If there’s one great thing about it, that is its quality. If you visit a coffee shop and see a good, experienced barista do their job, you’ll be amazed. You’ll notice the barista weigh the coffee beans on a scale, grind them, and pour them with a unique technique.

Seeing how we do the preparation is one thing; having the actual aroma and taste of the speciality coffee is another. You’ll savour something like no other with it's rich flavour notes and strong aroma, no matter what your order is. This is because the industry pays particular attention to the quality of the coffee beans, the use of proper tools and machines, and the right preparation techniques.
